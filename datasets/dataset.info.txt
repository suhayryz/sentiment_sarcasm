Dataset Information: bilingual_sentiment_sarcastic.csv

Dataset: Comments acquired from Malay Facebook public pages during 23/10/2015 - 12/11/2015 based on economic related posts.

Dataset annotation: 3 annotators (majority vote):
(i) sent_majority: the sentiments of the comments (positive and negative) *neutral were omitted. 
(ii) sarc_majority: the existence of sarcasm on positive and negative comments only (sarcastic and non-sarcastic).

Fleiss’s kappa inter annotator agreement score:
(1) Sentiment (sent_majority) = 0.71 (substantial agreement) 
(2) sarcasm (sarc_majority) = 0.47 (moderate agreement)
